## Highs & Lows

During Hacktoberfest, I encountered several highs and lows. Some of my biggest accomplishments included successfully making my first pull request, which was truly a light-bulb moment. It was a fulfilling experience to be part of open-source projects and make meaningful contributions.

However, it wasn't always smooth sailing. I faced challenges along the way, including conflicts with other contributions. These moments taught me the importance of adaptability, communication, and creative problem-solving. It's incredible how the open-source community comes together to help one another overcome hurdles.

- [Conflict Resoled #21](https://github.com/daoodaba975/add.me.in/pull/21)

## Growth

Before Hacktoberfest 2023, my skillset was somewhat limited, and I had minimal experience contributing to open-source projects. However, during this event, I've seen substantial growth in my skills and knowledge. I've gained a better understanding of version control, collaboration through pull requests, and project organization.

My learning and career goals have evolved as well. I now have a deeper appreciation for the power of open source in driving innovation and positive change in the tech industry. I'm more motivated than ever to continue contributing and learning from the global community of developers.
